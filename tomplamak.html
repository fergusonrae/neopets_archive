» Splices

What is a splice?
Splices are graphics that are really two pictures put, or sliced, together. A form of blending. Call it what you will, these graphics can be pretty cute. ^-^

I've made splices of Neopets for your use on your userlookup, guild, shop, pet page, whatever! Copy and paste the codes you want, where you want, and behold the mixed pets. :D They are organized by color, rather than species.

Also, please keep credit on. I see you with a splice with no credit = wall of shame!!
